I am a 19 year old woman from Seattle that was raised without a clue as to what financial stability looked like. I was living in proverty most of my childhood, and moving in with my grandmother at age 14 was the closest I ever got to getting out of it. I recently moved out of my Grandma's house and into my first apartment. Over the past year, I couldn't help but worry about what I was going to do with my life. I felt that I had no valuable skills, and while I was eager to learn, I couldn't figure out where to start. I kept coming up with all kinds of career paths that I was interested in, but nothing stuck, and I never took action. This all changed when I met my friend Rozey. Rozey was the first person to take the time to give me really valuable advice. He made me believe that I had a chance. He provided me with the resources I needed to get started, I told my Grandma all about it, and she purchased The Complete 2022 Web Development Bootcamp by Angela Yu for me. This $119 purchase changed my life. I immediately began to enjoy coding and everything that came with Web Development. That's when I knew I was on the path to a job that not only would I enjoy, but would provide me financial freedom, something that I've never experienced before.
